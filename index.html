The Samepage Care Transformation Program – a turnkey, configuration solution

Samepage’s Care Transformation Program is an evidence-driven patient-centered approach to provide optimal chronic care in primary care settings.

The solution, consisting of three modules, Goal-Driven Care Model Transformation, the Change Path SaaS technology, and the Ongoing Improvement Program, allows providers to use their existing staff and systems to provide consistent, evidence-based, cost-reducing care.
  • Based on the collaborative care model, including problem-solving therapy, the Chronic Care Model, and Collaborative Depression Care principles
  • Automates core workflows with a SaaS-based technology platform
  • Provides ongoing access to Samepage professionals for continued program improvement
